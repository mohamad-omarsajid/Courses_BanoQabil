# 0.3 AI as a Design Brain

!!! info "Why this lesson?"
    AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude are like a patient helper who never gets
    tired. They can give you ideas, name your colors, write short text, and tell
    you honestly what is weak in your design. This lesson shows you how to use
    them well.

    *(Roman Urdu: ChatGPT aur Claude jaise AI tools ek aise madadgar ki tarah
    hain jo kabhi nahi thakte. Yeh tumhe ideas dete hain, colors ke naam batate
    hain, chhota text likhte hain, aur tumhare design ki kamiyan saaf saaf
    batate hain.)*

## What you will learn

- How to brainstorm poster ideas with AI
- How to name colors with AI
- How to write short copy with AI
- How to ask AI for honest critique of your design

## What AI is, and what it is not

**AI** (Roman Urdu: aisa computer program jo tumhare sawal ka jawab insaan ki
tarah baat kar ke deta hai) is a smart helper, not a designer who replaces you.
It gives you a starting point. You still make the choices and do the work.

Think of it as a friend who knows a lot but sometimes guesses wrong. You take
the good ideas and leave the rest.

## Brainstorm poster ideas

When your head is empty, AI can fill the page with options fast. Ask for many
ideas, then pick.

!!! example "A prompt for ideas"
    "I am making a poster for a small tea shop in Sahiwal. Give me five
    different poster concepts. For each one, tell me the main message, the mood,
    and the colors."

You will not use all five. But one or two will spark something. That is the
point.

## Name your colors

A good color name helps you and your client talk about a design. AI is great at
this.

!!! example "A prompt for color names"
    "Here are three colors: a deep green `#0fab95`, a warm cream `#f5efe0`, and
    a dark brown `#3b2a1a`. Give each a nice name and tell me what feeling it
    gives."

Now instead of "that green one", you can say "the deep jade green". It sounds
clear and professional.

## Write short copy

**Copy** (Roman Urdu: design par likha hua text, jaise heading ya tagline) is
the words on your poster or post. AI can write short, punchy lines for you.

!!! example "A prompt for copy"
    "Write three short taglines for a tea shop, each under six words. Make them
    warm and simple."

Pick the one you like. Then change a word or two so it feels like your own.

## Ask for honest critique

You cannot upload a screenshot in every tool, so describe your design in words.
Be clear and specific.

!!! example "A prompt for critique"
    "I made a poster. The background is dark green. The shop name is in white at
    the top in a bold font. There is a small tea cup photo in the middle, and
    the address is in tiny gray text at the bottom. What is weak about this, and
    how can I improve it?"

AI will point out problems, like the address being too small to read. That is
free, honest feedback you can act on.

!!! warning "Two rules you must follow"
    1. Do not pass AI work off as your own to a client without telling them.
       Be honest about what AI helped with. Trust matters more than looking
       clever.
    2. AI can be confidently wrong. It may invent a fact or a wrong color rule.
       Always check important facts yourself before you trust them.

!!! tip "tl;dr"
    AI is a helper, not your replacement. Use it to brainstorm poster ideas,
    name your colors, write short copy, and get honest critique by describing
    your design in words. Always check its facts, and be honest with clients
    about what AI did.

## Knowledge check

1. Name one good way to use AI in design.
2. Why should you describe your design in detail when asking for critique?
3. What are the two rules you must follow when using AI for client work?
4. Should you trust everything AI tells you? Why or why not?

??? success "Urdu mein chhoti wazahat"
    AI ek madadgar hai, tumhari jagah nahi leta. Tum isse poster ke ideas,
    colors ke naam, chhota text aur design par imaandar raye le sakte ho.
    Critique ke liye apna design lafzon mein achhi tarah bayan karo. Client ko
    sach batao ke AI ne kya madad ki, aur har zaroori baat khud check karo
    kyunke AI ghalat bhi ho sakta hai.

## Optional assignment

Pick any small shop near you. Ask an AI for three poster concepts for it. Pick
your favourite. Then describe that concept in words and ask the AI to critique
it. Write down one change you will make based on the critique.
