# Graphic Design with UI/UX

Welcome. This is a free course that teaches you graphic design and UI/UX from
zero.

You do not need any experience. You do not need to pay. You do not need to keep
up with a class. You start when you want, and you go at your own speed.

!!! info "What you will be able to do"
    By the end of this course you can design posters, logos, social media posts,
    and your first app screens. Real work you can show to family, friends, or a
    first client.

    *(Roman Urdu: Is course ke baad tum posters, logos, social media posts, aur
    apni pehli app screens design kar sako ge. Asli kaam jo tum logon ko dikha
    sako.)*

## How this course works

- **Free forever.** No fees, no card, no hidden plan.
- **Self-paced.** No deadlines and no fixed timing. One small lesson at a time.
- **For absolute beginners.** We explain every new word the first time we use it.
- **English and Roman Urdu.** Hard words get a short Roman Urdu meaning in brackets.
- **You make real things.** Every chapter ends with something you designed yourself.

## What you need

1. A laptop or computer (a phone works for some tools, but design is easier on a computer).
2. An internet connection.
3. A free Google account.

We start with free tools, and we use free trials only where we must.

## The plan

The course has seven chapters. Each chapter has a few short lessons.

| Chapter | What you learn |
|---|---|
| 0. Getting Ready | Files, cloud drives, and how to learn design well |
| 1. Design Fundamentals | Hierarchy, color, type, and layout |
| 2. Canva | The easiest tool, for fast real designs |
| 3. Adobe Photoshop | Editing photos and raster art |
| 4. Adobe Illustrator | Logos and icons with vectors |
| 5. Affinity by Canva | A free, full design suite |
| 6. Figma + UI/UX | App screens, prototypes, and UX basics |

You are at the start. That is the perfect place to be.
