# Chapter 1: Design Fundamentals

<p class="byline" markdown>Muhammad Umar Sajid · Trainer at Bano Qabil Sahiwal · Front End Development and Graphic Design</p>

Good design is not decoration. It is communication. A good poster, app, or
ad sends a clear message and guides your eye to the right place. This chapter
teaches the four ideas behind almost every good design: hierarchy, color,
type, and layout.

*(Roman Urdu: Achi design sirf sajawat nahi hoti. Yeh ek paigham hai. Is chapter
mein tum design ke chaar buniyadi usool seekho ge: hierarchy, color, type, aur
layout.)*
