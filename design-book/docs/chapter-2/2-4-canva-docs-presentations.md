# 2.4 Canva Docs & Presentations

!!! info "Why this lesson?"
    By the end of this lesson you can make a slide deck, use Canva Docs for a
    simple document, present your slides, and export to PDF or PowerPoint.

    *(Roman Urdu: Is sabaq ke baad tum ek slide deck bana sako ge, simple
    document ke liye Canva Docs use kar sako ge, slides pesh kar sako ge, aur PDF
    ya PowerPoint mein export kar sako ge.)*

## What you will learn

- How to make a slide deck
- What Canva Docs is and when to use it
- How to present your slides
- How to export to PDF or PowerPoint

## Make a slide deck

A **slide deck** (Roman Urdu: slides ka set jo tum kisi ko dikhate ho) is a set of
slides you show one after another. People use it to explain an idea step by step.

To make one:

1. On the dashboard, search "Presentation".
2. Pick a template you like.
3. Each page is one slide. Change the words and photos on each.
4. To add a slide, click **Add page** at the bottom.

Keep each slide simple. One idea per slide. A few words, not a paragraph. The
slide should support what you say, not say everything for you.

!!! tip "Less text per slide"
    A common mistake is too much text on one slide. Use short lines and big words.
    If a slide feels crowded, split it into two slides.

## What is Canva Docs?

**Canva Docs** (Roman Urdu: Canva mein likhne wala simple document, jaise ek
report) is for writing documents, not slides. It works like a normal text page
where you type from top to bottom.

Use Canva Docs when:

- You are writing something to read, like a report, a letter, or notes.
- The words matter more than the layout.
- You do not need to stand and present it.

Use a presentation instead when:

- You will show it on a screen to people.
- You want one idea on each slide.
- You will speak while it shows.

So the rule is simple. Reading goes in a doc. Showing and speaking goes in a
presentation.

## Present your slides

When your deck is ready, you can present it right from Canva.

1. Click **Present** at the top right.
2. Pick **Presenter view** or full screen.
3. Use the arrow keys to move forward and back.
4. Press Escape to stop.

You do not need any other app. Canva shows your slides full screen for you.

## Export to PDF or PowerPoint

Sometimes you need to send your deck, not present it live. You can export it.

1. Click **Share**, then **Download**.
2. For a file anyone can open and read, choose **PDF Standard**.
3. To open it later in Microsoft PowerPoint, choose **PowerPoint (PPTX)**.
4. Click **Download**.

!!! example "Pick the right export"
    Sending it to someone to just read? Use PDF. Need to edit it in PowerPoint on
    another computer? Use PPTX. For a printed handout, use PDF Print like you
    learned in the last lesson.

!!! tip "tl;dr"
    A slide deck shows one idea per slide while you speak. Canva Docs is for
    documents you read, like reports. Present straight from Canva with the Present
    button and arrow keys. Export to PDF to share and read, or to PowerPoint to
    edit elsewhere.
