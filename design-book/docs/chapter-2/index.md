# Chapter 2: Canva

<p class="byline" markdown>Muhammad Umar Sajid · Trainer at Bano Qabil Sahiwal · Front End Development and Graphic Design</p>

Canva is the easiest way to make real designs fast. It is free, and it runs in your browser. You do not install anything. In this chapter you will make three real things: a social media post, a CV, and a slide deck.

*(Roman Urdu: Canva sab se aasaan tareeqa hai jaldi design banane ka. Yeh free hai aur browser mein chalta hai. Is chapter mein tum teen asli cheezein bana lo ge: ek social post, ek CV, aur ek slide deck.)*

Lessons in this chapter:

- 2.1 Canva Tour
- 2.2 Social Media Graphics
- 2.3 Posters, Flyers & CVs
- 2.4 Canva Docs & Presentations

!!! tip "No rush"
    Go slow. Do each lesson, then make the small piece it asks for. You learn design by making things, not by reading about them. There is no deadline here.
