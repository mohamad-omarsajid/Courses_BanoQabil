# Front-End Development, from zero

Salaam, and welcome. This is a free course that takes you from never having
written code to building real websites that you can put online and show the
world.

You do not need any experience. You do not need to pay. You do not need to keep
up with a class. You start when you want, and you move at your own speed.

## What this book is

This is the full written course for Front-End Development at Bano Qabil Sahiwal.
It teaches you to build modern websites with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind,
React, and more. By the end you will have a real ecommerce project and a personal
portfolio that you can show to an employer or a client.

The book stands on its own. If you attend the in-person Bano Qabil Sahiwal class,
this is your textbook. If you are studying from home in another city, you can
finish the whole thing without ever sitting in a classroom.

## How to use this book

- **Go in order.** Each chapter builds on the one before it.
- **Type the code yourself.** Do not copy and paste. Your hands need the practice.
- **Do the "Try this" steps.** Reading is not enough. You learn by doing.
- **Get stuck, then get unstuck.** Being stuck is normal. Every lesson shows you how to find your way out.
- **Take your time.** There is no deadline. A lesson a day is great progress.

Hard words get a dotted underline. Hover or tap them to see a short meaning, in
English and Urdu where it helps.

## What you'll be able to build

- A personal portfolio website
- A working ecommerce store with a cart and checkout
- Animated, modern pages that feel alive
- Sites you can deploy to a live URL for free

## What you need

1. A laptop or computer with Windows, macOS, or Linux.
2. An internet connection.
3. A free Google account and a free GitHub account (we set these up together).

That is all. Every tool in this course is free.
